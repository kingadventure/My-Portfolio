Coming Soon

This React App is still in progress

I have always wanted to keep up with the news. Knowing what's going on in the world and in my own neightborhood has always been very important to me. In the last year this has become more difficult with high political tensions and the not always hopeful updates on COVID-19. Everything is breaking news, and its breaking people's mental health. Although I still have my strong desire to stay informed, I would like to have a news platform that keeps mental health in mind while providing the same updates. I thought of "Zews" [zen-news]: headlines for healthy minds, a more encouraging way to stay up to date. Zews will be a compilation of articles written with mental health in mind. News outlets have known for quite a while that the more outrageous and emotion sparking headlines are the ones that sell. I would like to make my platform a place that is more focused on conveying the story and information in a way that is sustainable to read consistently. The platform will focus on having a forward direction with every story and unity in mind. This is not to censore any information, just to write it in a way that can leave the reader more hopeful and calm than the traditional articles we see. Zews will also compile a range of articles including some that may not be as highlighted with most American news outlets such as international stories, updates in science that are often overlooked, and community leaders and accomplishments. Zews will be a bipartisan platform emphasizing common ground instead of harmful divisions.

Zews is still in progress but is updated regularly. Please email me at kaelin@kingadventure.com if you wish to be an early subscriber!
